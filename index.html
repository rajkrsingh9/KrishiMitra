About NeerVaani

Welcome to NeerVaani!

Agriculture sustains the world, yet farmers struggle with unpredictable weather, inefficient irrigation, and limited access to real-time soil and climate data. Traditional methods rely on guesswork, leading to low yields, excessive water consumption, and environmental degradation. NeerVaani is an AI-driven precision agriculture platform that eliminates uncertainty by integrating remote sensing, IoT-powered real-time monitoring, and advanced predictive analytics. Our goal is to empower farmers with data-driven insights for smart crop selection, optimized irrigation, and sustainable farming practices—boosting productivity while reducing environmental impact..

AI-Powered Crop Prediction

Smart Crop Recommendation System Predicts the most suitable crops based on real-time environmental conditions. Uses climate data, soil properties, water availability, humidity, pH values, temperature, and topography. Water Scarcity Index ensures sustainable farming. Eliminates manual soil testing for N, P, K—NeerVaani makes data-driven decisions automatically.

Market-Driven Crop Selection

Suggests crops based on demand forecasting for economic profitability. Uses historical pricing data, supply-demand analytics, and government policies to predict future market trends.

Regional Water Insights

Get detailed reports on your water usage and explore surface and groundwater availability in your region to make informed, sustainable choices

Precision Irrigation Scheduler (A Key Differentiator)

Provides dynamic irrigation schedules specifying when, how often, and how much water to use. Uses real-time weather forecasts, soil moisture levels, and rainfall predictions to prevent overwatering and reduce water wastage. IoT-based smart systems enable automated irrigation control.
